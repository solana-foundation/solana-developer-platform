title: Ramps
description: Fiat on-ramps and off-ramps — let users buy crypto with fiat or cash out from a wallet.
---

SDP wraps several ramp providers behind two endpoints: `POST /v1/payments/ramps/onramp/execute` (fiat → crypto) and `POST /v1/payments/ramps/offramp/execute` (crypto → fiat). Each call returns a provider-hosted redirect URL that you hand the end-user; your backend tracks status by polling the returned reference.
