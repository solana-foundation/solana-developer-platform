title: Payouts and disbursements
description: Batched outbound transfers, idempotency, and reconciling payout runs with SDP.

Payouts are batches of outbound transfers — marketplace seller payouts, payroll, affiliate disbursements. SDP handles each payment as an individual `POST /v1/payments/transfers` call with an idempotency key, and `GET /v1/payments/transfers?direction=outbound` gives you the reconciliation view.

## Reconciling a run
