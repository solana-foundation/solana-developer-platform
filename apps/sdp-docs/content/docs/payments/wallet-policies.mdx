---
title: Wallet policies
description: Destination allowlist and transfer limits per custody wallet.
---

Wallet policies let you constrain how a custody wallet can send funds: a destination allowlist (only these addresses), a maximum per-transfer amount, and a maximum daily amount. Policies are managed via `GET` and `PUT` on `/v1/payments/wallets/{walletId}/policies`.

_This page is being authored as part of HOO-439. Sections below will cover:_

## When to use policies

Treasury operations, automated payout wallets, compliance-required limits.

## Destination allowlist

Adding / removing approved destinations; enforcement at transfer time.

## Transfer limits

`maxTransferAmount` (per-tx) and `maxDailyAmount` (rolling 24h) semantics.

## Updating policies

`PUT /v1/payments/wallets/{walletId}/policies` — full-replace semantics, audit trail.

How wallet policies compose with org-level compliance screening.
