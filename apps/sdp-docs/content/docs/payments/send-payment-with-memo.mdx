---
title: Payment with memo
description: Attach an order ID, invoice number, or customer reference to an SDP transfer.
---

Memos let you carry an order ID, invoice number, or customer reference alongside a payment so your indexer can reconcile each on-chain transfer back to your product database. The `memo` field on `POST /v1/payments/transfers` (and its `/prepare` variant) accepts an arbitrary UTF-8 string and is preserved on the transfer record.

## When to use a memo

Common patterns: order IDs, invoice numbers, end-customer references.
